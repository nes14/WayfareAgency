About

Wayfare was launched across Australasia, and worldwide, in September 2015. Wayfare is a print and digital magazine distributed to those with a love of travel, culture and adventure.

The magazine is a combination of stunning photography and insightful writing with features focused on the wider experience of visiting a place – the people, the history, the wildlife and the landscapes that make a place what it is. Our mission is to present insightful, intelligent travel content to inspire the next trip, adventure or exploration.

Wayfare readers are knowledgeable and interested in the world around them. They search for meaning and enjoy learning about the history, culture and geography of the places they visit. Readership is continually growing with our print subscribers predominantly based in Australia and New Zealand, while the majority of our digital subscribers are based around the globe.
